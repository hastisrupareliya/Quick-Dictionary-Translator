Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Quick Dictionary + Translator (“the Extension”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles data.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. All processing of highlighted words for definitions and translations is done locally in your browser.

Permissions

The Extension requests minimal permissions necessary to function:

  • Storage: To remember your preferred translation language.
  • ActiveTab: To access the currently active page so highlighted words can be processed.
  • Scripting: To inject content scripts for dictionary and translation popups.

The Extension does not transmit any highlighted text or website data to external servers.

Third-Party Sharing

No user data is shared with third parties under any circumstances.
