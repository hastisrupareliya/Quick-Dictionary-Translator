FAQ

Q: How do I use the extension?
A: Highlight any word on a webpage, and the popup will show definitions, examples, and translations.

Q: Can I change the language?
A: Yes, use the language dropdown in the popup to select your preferred language.

Q: Does this extension collect my data?
A: No, all processing happens locally. No personal data is collected or transmitted.
